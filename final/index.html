We exist because of
CO₂

Carbon dioxide, a greenhouse gas
that works to trap heat on Earth.
It helps Earth hold the energy it
receives from the Sun so it doesn't
all escape back into space. If it
weren't for carbon dioxide, Earth's
oceans would be frozen solid.

We will be extinct because of
CO₂

Carbon dioxide levels today are
higher than at any point in at least
the past 800,000 years. Human
activities such as deforestation and
burning fossil fuels have increased
atmospheric CO₂ concentration by
more than a third since the
Industrial Revolution began.

Majority of energy used in home
electronics is while they are turned
off but yet plugged in.

Think about every electronic item
you have plugged in. Every TV,
blender, coffee grinder, phone
charger and hair dryer can be
costing you not only money but
also our environment.
