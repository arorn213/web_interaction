WEBSITE AS A PLANT

The reading by Laurel Schwulst, was really interesting
especially the way in which she metaphorically explains
what a website is.
I was most drawn to her reference of a website as a plant.
Her expression of how our websites require patience and
creativity to evolve and be successful, makes me
question that how can I as a designer express my practice
and process through mediums of web interaction and
to what extent will this class aid me on those subjects?
